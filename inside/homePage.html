    About Me

    Born in a vibrant and bustling city on the northeastern side of the Pacific Ocean, I am Quang Tuan Tran—Tuan being my first name, Quang my middle name, and Tran my last name. In the spirit of 'When in Rome, do as the Romans do,' I embraced the name Thomas, inspired by the inventor of Thomas Edison. Thriving in my city of origin, I embarked on a transformative journey to Canada, driven by the ambition to pursue my dreams. Throughout my academic tenure at NBCC and outside NBCC, I meticulously honed my skills in a diverse array of programming languages and frameworks.



    In addition to my technical pursuits, I find joy in the creative realms of UI design and digital art, which I actively engage in as a passionate hobby. This avocation allows me to blend technical precision with artistic flair, contributing to a well-rounded skill set. I believe that the fusion of technology and design not only enhances the user experience but also adds a layer of aesthetic appeal to every project. This dual proficiency positions me as a versatile professional capable of bridging the gap between functionality and visual elegance.
