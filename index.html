About Me

Hailing from a dynamic city nestled on the northeastern shores of the Pacific Ocean, I am Trần Quang Tuấn. My journey in the technological realm is twofold: aspiring to be a game developer while simultaneously exploring the intricacies of full-stack web development. My academic and personal endeavours have allowed me to gain proficiency in a diverse spectrum of programming languages and frameworks.
From an early age, I was immersed in the world of gaming. Video games, an integral part of my life, have fueled my passion and shaped my aspiration to become a game developer. The insights and experiences I’ve gathered as a gamer infuse a unique perspective into my development process, enabling me to create engaging and immersive gaming experiences.
In addition to game development, I’ve discovered a fascination with website development. This has evolved into a delightful pastime, further expanding my skill set and providing an avenue for creative expression in the digital world. This amalgamation of gaming passion, web development interest, and diverse technical skills positions me uniquely in the tech industry, ready to make a significant impact.



From a young age, I was drawn to the world of art. My childhood was filled with hours spent drawing, painting, and experimenting with pottery. These early experiences sparked a passion in me that extended beyond traditional art forms and into the digital realm. Today, I find joy in the creative realms of digital art, which I actively engage in as a passionate hobby. This avocation allows me to blend technical precision with artistic flair, contributing to a well-rounded skill set. I believe that the fusion of technology and art not only enhances the user experience but also adds a layer of aesthetic appeal to every project. This dual proficiency, rooted in my lifelong love for art, positions me as a versatile professional capable of bridging the gap between functionality and visual elegance.
